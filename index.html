user experience after an era of disruption. Since 2020 the world has shifted. Accelerated trends have made

ahead. Across product, ecommerce, and marketing, HOTSAUCE will
give you the tools you need to evolve with these changes. Over two days, we'll share inspirational stories, dive deep into
empathy, and strengthen our connection with what our users
truly need.

FAQs

  • Who is this for?

    HOTSAUCE is for anyone working in marketing, product, UX design, and ecommerce but if you own a website or product you want to improve, you'll also find it useful. The aim is to inspire you by giving you the tools you need to empathize with your audience and improve your digital experience.

  • Is the venue accessible?

    We strive to make our events accessible to everyone. Please contact us for specific accessibility needs or questions.

  • How sustainable are events at Javits Center?

    The Javits Center is committed to sustainability. They have various initiatives in place to reduce their environmental impact. You can find more information on their website.

  • What's included in the ticket price?

    Your ticket includes access to all sessions, networking opportunities, and catering throughout the event.

  • What's the dress code?

    The dress code is casual and comfortable. We encourage you to wear what makes you feel best.

  • Where can I stay?

    There are many hotels near the Javits Center. We recommend booking in advance, especially if you are attending during peak season.

  • I've bought a ticket but can no longer attend, what do I do?

    Please contact us as soon as possible if you can no longer attend. We can discuss options for transferring your ticket or issuing a refund.
